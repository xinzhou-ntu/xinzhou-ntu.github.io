Short Bio.

Xin Zhou is a research fellow in EEE at Nanyang Technological University, co-advised by Dr. Zhang Jie and Dr. Sun Aixin. He received Ph.D in Informatics from Kyoto University in 2016 under the supervision of Prof. Toru Ishida. His research interests are data mining (DM), recommender system (RecSys), knowledge graph (KG), software engineering (SE) and reputation system (RepSys).
