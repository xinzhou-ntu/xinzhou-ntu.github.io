Short Bio

Zhishuai Zhang is a 3rd-year PhD student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Dr. Alan Yuille. His research interests are computer vision and machine learning. He is a member of Computational Cognition, Vision, and Learning.

Before that, he obtained B.E. in Computer Science at University of Science and Technology of China in 2016. He visited Johns Hopkins University in 2016 as a visiting undergraduate hosted by Dr. Alan Yuille.
